About Studio

Where Passion Meets Creativity.

At Vhoze Picstudio, we’re more than just photographers – we’re storytellers. Founded by Hamza Shabbir , our company was born out of a love for capturing the beauty in life’s fleeting moments. We take pride in creating images that tell a story, preserve memories, and evoke emotions long after the moment has passed.

Our team is made up of passionate, experienced photographers who share a vision: to provide exceptional photography that goes beyond just pictures. We focus on making every session personal, meaningful, and fun – whether it’s a wedding, family portrait, corporate event, or branding photoshoot.


Let’s Create Something Beautiful Together. Contact us today to learn more about how we can capture your memories.
